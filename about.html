Where We Came From

17 Years in the Making

OmniOne was officially established in Uluwatu, Bali in 2022 by a team of 8 whole systems designers — but the roots go back much further.

It began with a project called Green Earth City, approved by the Minister of Sustainability in Costa Rica. When it became clear the world wasn't quite ready, a deeper, more foundational design process began.

In 2014, a group called G.A.I.A. (Global Alliance of Intentional Agreements) brought together 40 collaborators over 3 years to design the Universal Agreement Fields — a shared language for all who are dedicated to a full-potential future.

These agreement fields grew over the years to include DAO Operations, Space Agreements, a solid set of values, and the birth of what would become one of the world's first Teal Whole Living System Blueprint Design Factories.

The Founding Insight

Most efforts to build whole living systems try to grab existing organizations and sew them together. That's like sewing organs to create a human.

OmniOne grows the embryo. Then the child. Then the full human. This is what "Whole Living Systems" actually means — and it's why we believe we will be the ones to achieve the New Paradigm.

Why It's Different

If the economy doesn't match the governance, any new system defaults back to the old one — because fiat money is attached to decisions. Without creating a new economy, we cannot create new governance.

OmniOne builds both simultaneously. That's the unfair advantage.

The System

An Ecosystem, Not an Organization

OmniOne is built on the Teal Paradigm — three core characteristics that define the new way of organizing human systems.

🌀

Self-Organized

No hierarchy, no force, no coercion. Every action emerges from free will, sovereignty, and a strong sense of shared purpose. People contribute because they want to, not because they have to.

🌿

Whole Living Systems

The ecosystem is designed as a single living organism — not a collection of departments. When one part thrives, all parts benefit. When one part struggles, the system provides support.

🎯

Purpose Driven

Every node in the ecosystem has a clear purpose that aligns with the whole. Sense-making tools, agreement fields, and the ACT process ensure coherence without centralized control.

Our Metaphor

From the Finite Game
to the Infinite Game

In a finite game, we play within the boundaries. In an infinite game, we play with the boundaries.

OmniOne gives its tribe members the tools to build new spaces — new containers — for human energy, time, and experience to grow and develop. We provide the walls (boundaries, processes, guidelines) so that when the dam releases, the energy flows cleanly into its new space with no blocks.

The river is the energy. The river banks are the agreement fields. ETHOS is the blueprint. OmniOne is the tools and space for the buildout.

Once the new spaces are ready, the energy flows — completely, with as little waiting as possible, as complete a roadmap as possible.
