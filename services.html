Organizational Structure

ETHOS is OmniOne's organizational certification pathway — a Teal-based governance design that allows teams, communities, and organizations to operate with coherence, sovereignty, and purpose at any scale.

When your organization becomes an ETHOS, you get:

  • Access to OmniOne's full agreement field infrastructure
  • Consent-based decision-making frameworks (ACT)
  • Values alignment tools and coordination systems
  • Matching with aligned partner organizations
  • Entry into the OmniOne ecosystem network
  • 10–15% efficiency gains through pre-matched coordination
